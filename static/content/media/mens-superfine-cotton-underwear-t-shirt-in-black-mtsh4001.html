Our underwear T-Shirt is made from the same terrifically soft Q82 cotton jersey that we use for our classic outerwear T-Shirt, but is cut with more length in the body and a slightly wider fit. Add it to your loungewear essentials or wear it as a layering piece during the colder months.

Our signature Q (for Quality) 82 fabric is a fine jersey fabric made from long-staple cotton fibres that are twisted together to create a strong, smooth yarn that won’t tangle through being worn or washed. This twist makes it an incredibly clean yarn, though it is still passed over a flame to remove overhanging ends. The yarn is then double-spun to remove any remaining impurities.

It might be said that this is a rather laborious process, but we think that the resulting jersey that is not only long-lasting but also the lightest and the softest that we have ever felt, is worth every effort.
